隐私政策 · 时刻保鲜

最后更新：2026 年 4 月 20 日

感谢你使用「时刻保鲜」（以下简称"本 App"）。我们非常重视你的隐私，本政策说明我们如何处理信息。

1. 我们收集什么

我们不收集、不上传、不储存你的任何个人数据。你在本 App 中录入的所有物品信息（名称、日期、分类、备注等）全部存放在你设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器。

2. 权限说明

  • 相机：仅在你主动使用扫码或拍照录入功能时调用，用于识别商品条码或拍摄物品照片。照片和识别结果仅保存在你本地设备，我们不会上传。
  • 通知：用于发送你设置的到期提醒。所有提醒在设备本地生成与触发。

3. 第三方服务

本 App 不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

订阅与内购功能由 Apple App Store 提供，相关交易信息由 Apple 按其隐私政策处理，我们无法访问你的支付信息。

4. 数据安全

由于数据仅存于你的设备本地，请自行做好设备备份与保护。卸载 App 将会删除所有已录入的数据。

5. 儿童隐私

本 App 适用于所有年龄（4+），不专门面向儿童，也不会收集任何可识别儿童身份的信息。

6. 政策更新

若未来我们需要更新本隐私政策，将在本页面公布变更内容，并在 App 内提示。

7. 联系我们

如有任何疑问或建议，请联系：

Privacy Policy · FreshTime

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Thank you for using FreshTime ("the App"). Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how your information is handled.

1. What We Collect

We do not collect, upload, or store any of your personal data. All the items you enter in the App (name, dates, categories, notes, etc.) are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any server.

2. Permissions

  • Camera: Used only when you actively use barcode scanning or photo input. Photos and recognition results stay on your device. We never upload them.
  • Notifications: Used to deliver the expiry reminders you configure. All reminders are generated and triggered locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Subscriptions and in-app purchases are handled by the Apple App Store. Transaction data is processed by Apple under its own privacy policy; we have no access to your payment details.

4. Data Security

Because your data is stored only on your device, please back up your device as appropriate. Uninstalling the App will delete all the data you entered.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is intended for all ages. It does not target children specifically and does not collect any information that could identify a child.

6. Policy Updates

If we update this privacy policy in the future, we will post the changes on this page and notify users within the App.
