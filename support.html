常见问题

Q: 数据会上传到云端吗？

A: 不会。所有数据都保存在你的设备本地，卸载 App 会一并删除。详见隐私政策。

Q: 如何订阅 Pro？

A: 打开 App → 设置 → FreshTime Pro，选择月付 / 年付 / 终身方案后确认购买。

Q: 如何取消订阅？

A: 打开 iPhone「设置」→ 点击顶部你的 Apple ID → 订阅 → 选择 FreshTime → 取消订阅。订阅会在当前周期结束后停止。

Q: 如何恢复已购买的订阅？

A: 在 App 的订阅页面点击「恢复购买」按钮即可，确保使用购买时的同一 Apple ID。

Q: 订阅自动续费规则？

A: 订阅会在当前周期结束前 24 小时自动续费，除非你提前关闭。费用会从绑定的 Apple ID 账户扣除。

Q: 换了新 iPhone，数据怎么迁移？

A: 可通过 Pro 功能中的「数据导出 / 导入」进行迁移，或使用 iCloud 整机备份恢复。

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data uploaded to the cloud?

A: No. All data is stored locally on your device and will be removed when you uninstall the app. See our Privacy Policy.

Q: How do I subscribe to Pro?

A: Open the app → Settings → FreshTime Pro, then choose Monthly / Yearly / Lifetime and confirm the purchase.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

A: On your iPhone, go to Settings → tap your Apple ID at the top → Subscriptions → FreshTime → Cancel Subscription. It will stop renewing after the current period ends.

Q: How do I restore a previous purchase?

A: Tap "Restore Purchases" on the subscription page inside the app. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase.

Q: How does auto-renewal work?

A: Subscriptions automatically renew 24 hours before the current period ends unless turned off. The charge will be applied to your Apple ID account.

Q: How do I migrate data to a new iPhone?

A: Use the "Export / Import Data" feature included with Pro, or restore from a full iCloud backup.
